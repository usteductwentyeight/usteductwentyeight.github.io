BIOGRAPHY

Assoc. Prof. Evelyn O. Ladines was employed for 15 years at the Food and Nutrition Research Institute (FNRI), Department of Food Science and Technology (DOST) doing research on food compositional studies specializing on instrumental methods. She has been a trainor for new B.S. Chemistry graduates in Food Analysis for 10 years while at FNRI and been teaching chemistry subjects part-time. Currently, she finds joy in teaching chemistry applied to foods: preferably the assessment of physico-chemical properties and nutritional profile.
