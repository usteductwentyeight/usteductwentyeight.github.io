BIOGRAPHY

Ms. Essence Jeanne Logan is a professional teacher and food industry consultant. She hold a degree in MS Food Science from the University of Santo Tomas. She is currently working as a college professor handling courses related to Food Science and Technology such as Food Processing, Food Safety, Food Microbiology, Sensory Evaluation, Food Packaging and Food Chemistry, at the same time facilitating seminars about the said courses and conducting food safety audits in small to large-scale industries.
