BIOGRAPHY

Ms. Glorilee Anne Berbano, M.A. Ed., LPT, finished her Bachelor’s degree in University of Santo Tomas. She took up Elementary Education major in Special Education. In 2014, Ms. Berbano accomplished Master of Arts in Education from the Ateneo de Manila University. She is a practitioner in the field of Special Education wherein she specializes in inclusive education and teaching reading for students with exceptionalities. Currently, she is the Assistant Principal of Cradle of Learners Inc. (inclusive school for individualized education) and a part-time college instructor of University of Santo Tomas College of Education.
