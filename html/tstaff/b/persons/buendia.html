BIOGRAPHY

Maria Mercedes c. Buendia is a Professor at the College of Education, University of Santo Tomas, handling Educational Technology courses to Education students. She also teachers Computer and Principles of Teaching courses in the College of Education.

Ms. Buendia is a graduate of Bachelor of Secondary Education major in Computer Technology and Master of Arts in Education major in Educational Management both from the University of Santo Tomas. She co-authored Computer books for elementary level and Educational Technology 1 and 2 for Education program students. She facilitates training on e-Learning and the effective integration of technology in teaching.
