BIOGRAPHY

Associate Professor Eufemio Barcelon has been teaching Food Technology courses for over 2 decades. His teaching is focused mainly on the critical thinking in solving problems on Food Engineering in application to foods processing, innovation, food safety, among others. He has also extended his services as consultant in several food industries and instituttions. Several times, he is being invited as resource person/speaker in food trainings, sensory evaluation and food processing. He is a DOST Balik Scientist and a faculty member of the Graduate School MS Food Science program.
