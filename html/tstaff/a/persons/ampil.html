BIOGRAPHY

Roberto D. Ampil, PhD, is a full time faculty member of the University of Santo Tomas teaching Filipino (General Education and Majors) at the Faculty of Arts and Letters and College of Education. He finished his Doctorate in Filipino from the Philippine Normal University-Manila as CHED scholar. Dr. Ampil has lectured on the subject of discourse analysis, film analysis and Filipino language and culture in numerous local and international conferences. He is also the former chairperson of the Department of Filipino (2010-2015). Dr. Ampil was honored by the Division of City Schools-Manila and KAGUFIL with its Most Outstanding Teacher Award in 2001 and won 3rd place in Komisyon sa Wikang Filipino (KWF) Gawad Collantes National Essay Contest on the same year. He is an author and coordinator-editor to various High School and College textbooks.
