BIOGRAPHY

Assoc. Prof. Grace Azares has been teaching Chemistry courses for over 2 decades. Her teaching is focused mainly on the critical thinking in solving problems on Analytical and Physical Chemistry in application to foods and the environment, among others. Other than teaching, she has been tasked to prepare documents and organize procedures for accreditation of programs of the college. She has also extended her services as chair, co-chair or member of a number of committees, both academic and extra-curricular. Several times, she is being invited as judge/consultant in quiz contests, poster presentations and investigative projects on chemistry topics.
