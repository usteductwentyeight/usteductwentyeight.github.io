BIOGRAPHY

Dr. Rhodelia Mendoza completed her basic education at Guinayangan, Quezon, and her undergraduate degree in Secondary Education with majors in English and Filipino in 1973 at the Manuel Luis Quezon University, Manila. She obtained her M.A. Filipino degree in 1998 having been influenced by her mentor, Atty. Ponciano B.P. Pineda, former Chair of Komisyon sa Wikang Filipino. She attained her doctorate degree in Filipino in 2001. A competent and able educator, Dr. Mendoza instilled awareness and imbibed among her students respect for the Philippines’ native language, its beauty and importance. With decades of teaching experience, she has been an instrument for inculcating love of one’s language, country and people in the minds of the Filipino youth.
