BIOGRAPHY

Diane S. Mendoza, MSc, RND is an Instructor III at the Department of Nutrition and Dietetics of the University of Santo Tomas, where she handles professional nutrition courses such as Nutrition Therapy I and II, Assessment of Nutritional Status, Food and Nutrition Research, Basic Foods I, and Food Service Systems I. She earned her Bachelor’s degree in Nutrition and Dietetics at the University of Santo Tomas and obtained his Master’s degree in Clinical Nutrition at the Philippine Women’s University. Prior to being an academician, she was a clinical Dietitian, serving almost two years in a government hospital and three years in the University of Santo Tomas Hospital.
