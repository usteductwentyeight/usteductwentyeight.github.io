BIOGRAPHY

Prof. Dr. Allan B. de Guzman handles pedagogy, management and research courses at both graduate and undergraduate levels in the University of Santo Tomas. As a prolific writer in educational policy studies, adult learning and teacher education, he has extensively published a total of 140 articles in various ISI-listed journals and at the same time serves as editor, board member and reviewer in international journals. He has received various prestigious awards which included the 2007 SEAMEO-JASPER Research Award given by the Government of Canada and the Southeast Asian Ministers of Education Organization (SEAMEO), the 2006 National Research Council of the Philippines (NRCP)-Department of Science and Technology (DOST) Achievement Award and the 2011 Metrobank Foundation Search Most Outstanding Teacher Award in Higher Education, among others. He was the recipient of the 2104 Australian Awards Fellowship (AAF) on Curriculum Leadership at Queensland University of Technology, Brisbane, Australia. He is currently the Dean of the College of Education, University of Santo Tomas, Manila, Philippines. In 2017, he was appointed by President Rodrigo R. Duterte to the 7-member committee of the Teacher Education Council in the Philippines.
