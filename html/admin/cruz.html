BIOGRAPHY

Asst. Prof. Kashmer T. Cruz finished Bachelor of Science in Medical Technology, Cum Laude, in the University of Santo Tomas (UST) in 1996. She placed third in the 1996 Medical Technology Licensure Examinations. She started her teaching career in UST in 1996 and completed Master in Biology Education at the UST Graduate School in 2003. She also completed Bachelor of Science in Nursing at the Philippine College of Health Sciences in 2008 and passed the 2008 Licensure Examinations for Nurses. At present, she is pursuing her Doctorate Degree in Education majoring in Curriculum and Instruction at the UST Graduate School.

As a Registered Medical Technologist (RMT) and a Registered Nurse (RN) by profession but a teacher by passion, Asst. Prof. Cruz handles courses in several disciplines such as Chemistry, Biological Sciences and Health Sciences. She also served as the Student Welfare and Development Board (SWDB) Coordinator for the UST College of Education from 2013 to 2016. Currently, she is the College Secretary of the UST College of Education.
